Looking for love online?

Follow one lonely, divorced man as he tries to find love online. These are the true stories of the women he meets on his journey. Each story is told in a matter-of-fact manner style and entirely from his perspective.

This book is more about failure than it is about success. It's NOT a book about getting chicks or macking women successfully. Quite the opposite. In this book you'll see one man's (at times) humorous and (at other times)sad attempts to find love.

You'll learn many things you shouldn't do when dating online. You'll learn some things that do work successfully, but overall you'll get a realistic picture of the online dating scene, and it's not pretty'.
